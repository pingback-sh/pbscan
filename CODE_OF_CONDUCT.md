# Community conduct

Be respectful, technically constructive, and mindful that security research can affect real people and systems.

Do not publish private target data, credentials, callback captures, or uncoordinated vulnerability details in issues or discussions. Do not ask contributors to test systems without authorization. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and deliberate disruption are not accepted.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and restrict participation when needed to protect contributors, users, or affected organizations.
